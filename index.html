About Kaliveedu

Kaliveedu is the vibrant summer camp of Balayam Vihar, IIT Palakkad, designed to give children a joyful space to learn, play, explore, and grow. Through a rich mix of creative activities, games, arts, music, movement, and interaction, the camp nurtures confidence, curiosity, friendship, and expression.

Kaliveedu is not just a camp — it is a colourful experience where every child discovers new talents, creates warm memories, and enjoys a summer filled with happiness and energy.
